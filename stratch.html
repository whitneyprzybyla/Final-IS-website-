Facts about figure skating
  • It was the first winter sport to be included in the Olympic Games
  • The first ice skates were made with bone
  • The first figure skating club began in the 1740s in Edinburgh, Scotland
  • Skaters exceed 300 revolutions per minute
  • The most continous upright spins on ice skates on one foot is 115 revolutions
